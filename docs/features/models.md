# AI Models

Fabric supports a wide range of AI models from multiple providers.

## Supported Providers

### Anthropic

Claude models are known for thoughtful, nuanced responses.

- **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** - Latest and most capable
- **Claude 3 Opus** - Premium quality
- **Claude 3.5 Haiku** - Fast and efficient

### OpenAI

GPT models for general-purpose assistance.

- **GPT-4o** - Multimodal, fast
- **GPT-4 Turbo** - High capability
- **o1-preview** - Reasoning-focused

### Google

Gemini models with massive context windows.

- **Gemini 1.5 Pro** - 1M token context
- **Gemini 1.5 Flash** - Fast responses

### OpenRouter

Access 100+ models through a single API:

- Llama 3.1
- Mixtral
- DeepSeek
- And many more
